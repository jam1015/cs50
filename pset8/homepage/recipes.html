A recipe I like is to fry some onions up really good!

  1. You dice them up and salt them generously but not excessively.
  2. Then you add some grease to the pot; not butter because we'll be using high heat, but good options might be ghee, scmaltz, or olive oil.
  3. Then you put the onions in and mix it in with the fat!
  4. At first the onions will be steaming and boiling, but by the end whey they've shrunk, they'll be frying!
  5. Fry them slowly until they're completely browned; ideally just to the point when they'd start burning, but not burned at all; really takes discipline and judgement. Ok!that's the recipe.
  • Make sure you don't burn them
  • really great for any dish! put it in soup! put it on pizza, or just melted cheese on bread!
